## Approach

Leaf fragments (~20 mg) from herbarium sheets were extracted with a modified
CTAB protocol including a sorbitol pre-wash to remove polysaccharides. Libraries
were enriched with the Angiosperms353 universal bait set before sequencing.

## Outcome

Locus recovery correlated with specimen age and original drying speed. Recently
collected, fast-dried material gave near-complete gene sets; older sheets
recovered a usable but reduced subset.

## Caveats

Polysaccharide and polyphenol carry-over from some families inhibited library
prep; an additional clean-up step was needed for Malvaceae material.
